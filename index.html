Our story

The Damask tradition

For over two thousand years, Damask weaving has stood for patience and precision — a reversible weave, patterned on both faces of the cloth, that cannot be rushed or faked. Born in ancient China and perfected in Damascus, the technique travelled the medieval trade routes and became a byword for craftsmanship across continents.

DICIF takes its name and its discipline from that tradition. Where a weaver coordinates hundreds of threads with exacting care, we apply the same coordination to capital — deliberate, accountable, and built for value that compounds over years rather than weeks.

Charity

Beyond the balance sheet

Just as Damask weaving sustained entire families and communities for generations, DICIF aims to direct part of its impact toward shared community benefit — not only financial return.
